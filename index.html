Our Sacred History

Temple of Divine Glory

Established in 1892, this sacred temple serves as a beacon of spiritual enlightenment and divine grace. Thousands of devotees visit daily to experience the divine presence and seek inner peace.

Our Sacred Mission

To provide a sanctuary where souls can connect with the divine, experience spiritual transformation, and find lasting peace through devotion and service. We are dedicated to preserving ancient wisdom while serving humanity with compassion and love.

Our Divine Values

  • • Devotion and selfless service to the Divine
  • • Compassion and love for all living beings
  • • Preservation of sacred Vedic traditions
  • • Community service and charitable giving
